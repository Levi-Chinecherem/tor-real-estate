Our Land Verification System Overview

Our Land Verification System facilitates a seamless process for sellers, surveyors, and buyers to interact in the land transaction process. Here's how it works:

  • Sellers can register their lands and upload necessary documents and pictures for verification.
  • Surveyors verify the uploaded information by visiting the site and marking the property as verified.
  • Buyers can then browse verified properties, bid on them, and complete the purchase process.

Technologies Used: Django, Python, Tailwind CSS, PostgreSQL
